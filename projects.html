Our Projects

We value partnership and collaborations

1. Pilot project in Taft-Estrada, Manila

July 2017: Completed a proof-of-concept contactless traffic violations apprehension system in Taft Ave.-Estrada St. intersection, Malate, Manila, Philippines. It includes a CCTV network connected to a simple data center equipped with enterprise-grade computing server. The functionalities include vehicle detection, license plate recognition, and traffic violations (e.g., number coding).

2. FASTRAC CATCH-ALL

January 2019: The CATCH-ALL project received additional funding support from the Department of Science and technology through its “Funding Assistance for Spinoff and Translation of Research in Advancing Commercialization (FASTRAC)” program. The objective is to develop a fully operational and reliable video analytics system for traffic management. The focus is further development of contactless traffic violations apprehension using vehicle detection and license plate recognition technology.

3. CATCH-ALL System running in Cloud through AWS support

April 2020: ISI Inc received a business support grant from Amazon Web Services and Animo Lab TBI to develop a cloud-ready platform technology. Prior to the AWS support, ISI Inc. was developing a CATCH-ALL for On-Premises platform technology.

4. Traffic Impact Assessment in Roxas City, Capiz

April 2022: The CATCH-ALL project went to Roxas City, in coordination with the STARPLAN-VI project and the Local Government Unit of Roxas to test the CATCH-ALL Portable Traffic Counter and conduct traffic impact assessment and visual analytics.

5. Smart City Traffic Management in General Santos City

Upcoming: ISI Inc will have an upcoming project in General Santos City, South Cotabato, Philippines in coordination with Mindanao State University - General Santos City and the Local Government to implement the “iTwatcher: Intelligent Vision-Based 24/7 Traffic Watcher for General Santos” project
